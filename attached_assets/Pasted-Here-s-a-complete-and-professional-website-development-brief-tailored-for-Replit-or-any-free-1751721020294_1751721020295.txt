# 🧾 Assouan Restaurant Website – Full Development Guide

## 🧠 Goal

Design and build a **fully responsive**, **multilingual**, **performance-optimized** website for **Assouan Café-Pâtisserie-Restaurant**, based in Fès, Morocco. The site should reflect its **elegant, modern identity**, highlight its **menu and pastry offerings**, and encourage **online reservations**, **orders**, and **event/cake inquiries**.

---

| Feature               | Details                                                  |

## 🧭 SITE MAP (10 Pages + Core Components)

### 1. **Homepage**

* Hero video or photo banner
* Quick intro tagline: “Fès’ pastry landmark since 1990”
* Call-to-action buttons: **View Menu**, **Book a Table**
* Highlights: terrace, cakes, breakfast
* Today’s special / Ramadan info (auto-updated section)

### 2. **Menu**

* Tabs: *Breakfast · Hot Drinks · Juices · Viennoiserie · Savory · Desserts*
* Menu as **JSON-driven content** (editable from CMS)
* Filters: vegetarian, vegan, allergies
* Exportable PDF button

### 3. **Gallery**

* Grid layout: high-res photos
* API-based pull of Instagram #AssouanFes photos
* Lightbox viewer with descriptions

### 4. **Order Online**

* WhatsApp integration for quick orders
* “Reserve & Pick up” flow
* Optional Glovo or Jumia Food buttons
* Stripe / cash on pickup payment toggle

### 5. **Reserve a Table**

* Date/time picker
* Form (Name, Phone, Guests, Date, Time, Notes)
* Option: embed Calendly

### 6. **Custom Cakes / Events**

* Inquiry form: pick cake size, theme, date, photo upload
* Gallery of custom cakes and weddings

### 7. **About Us**

* Story since 1990
* Staff photos
* Brand philosophy (fresh, Moroccan, quality)

### 8. **Testimonials**

* Carousel of Google, Facebook, TripAdvisor reviews
* Star ratings
* “Leave your review” → Google redirect

### 9. **Blog (Optional)**

* Short news posts: New items, holidays, events
* SEO driver with categories

### 10. **Contact / Location**

* Google Map embed (centered on 4 Avenue Allal Ben Abdellah)
* Phone, WhatsApp, hours (auto from Google API)
* Quick buttons: “Call Now”, “Get Directions”
